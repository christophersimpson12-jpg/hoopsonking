8-Week Framework
THE
TRAINING.
01
Foundation & Evaluation
Footwork fundamentals, ball-handling baseline, athlete evaluation
02
Ball Handling & Attacking
Dribble combinations, change of direction, driving angles
03
Shooting Mechanics
Catch-and-shoot, off-the-dribble, shot off screens
04
Finishing at the Rim
Layup packages, contact finishing, floaters
05
Defense — On-Ball
Stance, positioning, lateral movement, forcing baseline
06
Defense — Off-Ball
Help-side positioning, rotations, weak-side awareness
07
Basketball IQ & Reads
Pick-and-roll reads, drive-and-kick, decision-making in live play
08
Competition & Evaluation
Full live play, end-of-session assessments, athlete progress review

Standards
THE
CODE.

Athletes

  • Arrive on time, ready to work every session
  • Respect coaches, teammates, and facility staff at all times
  • Give full effort — coachability is non-negotiable
  • No profanity, disrespectful language, or conduct that reflects poorly on the program
  • Academic performance matters — we are developing leaders, not just players

Parents & Guardians

  • Support your athlete with encouragement, not pressure
  • Respect the coaching process — trust the program
  • Do not coach from the sidelines during sessions
  • Direct concerns to a coach privately, not during a session
  • Punctual pickup is required at the end of every session
